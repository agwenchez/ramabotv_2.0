Best features of a Modern chatbot
Drag&drop Bot builder

You build your own ChatBot by drag and drop elements into your script building interface.

OTP Authentication

Ramabot has inbuilt OTP feature to confirm the contact data shared by the visitors, ensuring Data authentication.

Get leads 24/7

Ramabot sends you qualified and verified lead data on your phone numbers instantly 24/7, empowering your sales team.

Appointment Management

You can define your working days and time, decide the number of appointments you want to give out, and decide the length of the appointments.

Branch and Jump

You can branch your bot elements with our “RQ” Jump feature and publish the bot on your website with just a small, tiny script!

Data Delivery to your CRM

Ramabot has specific webhooks which allows you to get data collected and delivered directly to the CRM of your choice.
